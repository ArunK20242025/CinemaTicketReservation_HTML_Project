About the movie

Netrikann is a Tamil movie starring Ajmal Ameer, Nayanthara and Manikandan in prominent roles. It is a horror thriller directed by Milind Rau with K. Karthik Ganesh and R. D. Rajasekhar as cinematographers, forming part of the crew.
